About Us

We’re a small family-run catering business in the beautiful Engadine.

We love good food and great people.

Our specialty? Tasty burgers and homemade dishes made with fresh, local ingredients.

From birthdays to weddings, work events to casual get-togethers – we bring the flavor and good vibes.

You’ll usually find us in the same spot, ready with your favorite cheesy bites!

As a family, we care about the details – offering friendly service and food made with love.
